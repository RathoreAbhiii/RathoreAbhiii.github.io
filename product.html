Cavé Vintage Armagnac,
1943

Current Bid :

Rs 85,000.00

The 100-gallon Armagnac oak vats are made of age-old white oak trees
taken from the nearby Monlezun and Frêche forests. This variety of oak
makes for the best type of container for an outstanding brandy, and
gives it its very character. Evaporation of eau-de-vie, known as
“la part des anges”, takes place in the cool air of our cellars, which makes
for a natural reduction process. A permanent exchange occurs, in other
words, between the eau-de-vie, the fibers of the oak and the air.
